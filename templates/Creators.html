WHAT IS TAKLUBAN

Derived from the Filipino word 'TAKLUBAN', meaning 'to cover,' this tool is designed to filter and protect online conversations by detecting and masking inappropriate Tagalog, Cebuano, and Bikol languages.

TAKLUBAN, a tool developed for an undergraduate thesis, aims to ensure a respectful and safe environment across digital platforms, shielding usersâ€”especially Filipinos, from offensive content.
